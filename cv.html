About Me

I'm an ESL teacher, and aspiring full-stack web developer, with 7 years of teaching experience, and a huge passion for all things technology. I started my journey into the world, and teaching, at 18, and have since worked in 8 countries, learning a huge amount about life and the world on the way.

My current goal is to become a skilled full stack developer, increase my managerial experience for residential language courses, found an edutech startup, and to continue exploring this beautiful world.

Aspects

Residential Language Course Management

Last summer I was the Course Director at SUL Language School's Stover campus, where I was responsible for the successful running of the entire course. This year will see me returning in the same role at Ardmore's Eastbourne campus. In addition to this, I have 20 weeks residential course teaching & activity leader experience, including at Box Hill School, where this August I'll be returning for the 3rd year.

ESL Teaching

Fuelled by the desire to explore and be independant in life, I became an English teacher in Poland at the age of 18. Since then, I have accumulated 7 years of ESL teaching experience in a variety of nations and contexts, including at British Council Cairo and International House Cordoba. My qualifications include receiving an 'A' for CELTA and a 'Pass with Merit' for DELTA module 1 among others.

Online & Blended Learning Projects

I'm currently working on building 'Portalingo'; an online language learning portal for B2+ exam students. The site will aim to be an 'Udacity' of English language learning, feature video lessons, interactive activites and materials taken from interesting, real world, relevant materials.
